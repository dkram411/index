Welcome, Associate.

You've just joined Meridian Consumer Goods as a Senior Financial Analyst. The CFO has one hour before the Board meeting — and she needs answers.

Over the next 12 questions, you'll advise real business decisions using Cost-Volume-Profit (CVP) analysis — the framework that connects every unit sold to profit and loss. Get it right, and you'll earn your seat at the table.

📋 Learning Objectives
  • 01Distinguish fixed costs, variable costs, and mixed costs — and explain why it matters for every financial decision.
  • 02Calculate contribution margin (per unit and ratio) and interpret what it reveals about business scalability.
  • 03Determine break-even point in both units and sales dollars — and translate that into a real operational target.
  • 04Apply the margin of safety concept to assess downside risk.
  • 05Use CVP to evaluate strategic decisions: pricing changes, cost structure shifts, and target profit scenarios.
